Third section- difference between paddings, margins, and borders:

Paddings, margins, border, and the content are all part of the CSS box model.

Paddings create space between the content and the border.

The border can be a solid, dotted line of any thickness that surrounds the content and padding.

Margins create space between otheer contents.
